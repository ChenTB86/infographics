Two Tiers of Potential Claimants

The total market is split into two groups. The 'Core' market is based on the FCA's investigation into Discretionary Commission Arrangements (DCAs). The 'Speculative' market is a much larger group based on a wider court ruling, which is still under appeal.

Projected Claimant Uptake

Not everyone eligible will file a claim. Drawing parallels with the PPI scandal, our model assumes a 30% claimant uptake rate, which defines the Serviceable Obtainable Market and the ultimate scale of the opportunity.
